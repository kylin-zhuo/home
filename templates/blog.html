Take a break among the Utah rocks
April 15 2019

Lots of beautiful rocks, minerals and astonishing landcape of sedimentary rocks come from the state of Utah, which makes it a heaven for a rock-lover like me. There are 5 National Parks and 6 National Forests in Utah. The western part of the state yields obsidian, topaz, trilobites, agates, jaspers, and mineral specimens. The lower half of the east side of the state is particularly rich in rocks and minerals.

The fall colors in New England
Oct 23 2019

When time comes to the fall, or autumn, New England gets attractively famous for its glorious foliage as billions of leaves change from green to a kaleidoscope of colors. The air is crisp and cool, which makes it perfect for hiking, biking or just taking a classic fly-drive break along back roads, where farm stands are piled high with crunchy apples and orange pumpkins.
